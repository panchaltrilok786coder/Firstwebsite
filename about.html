About Us

HealthyLife Pharmacy was founded by Dr. Rajesh Patel in 2010. For over 16 years, we have been serving the Mumbai community with dedication and care.

Our store offers a wide range of products including prescription medicines, over-the-counter health products, vitamins, supplements, first aid kits, and personal care items.

Located conveniently at 123 Main Street, Mumbai, we ensure that quality healthcare products are easily accessible to everyone.
