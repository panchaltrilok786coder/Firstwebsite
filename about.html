Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Dr. Rajesh Patel, HealthyLife Pharmacy was created with a single mission: to provide the highest quality healthcare products while offering personalized care to the community. Over the past 16 years, we’ve grown into a trusted name in Mumbai for both prescription and over-the-counter medicines.

We believe healthcare is more than just medications—it’s about wellness, trust, and guidance. Our staff is dedicated to helping you choose the right products and maintaining your health with expert advice.
